Blackjack Makerthon!
---

Basic rules:

```
Player goes bust if over 21
Picture cards are worth 10 points
Ace is worth 1 or 11
Player is dealt minimum 2 cards
Player can continue to hit until he goes bust
```

Ronin Casino Rules
```
Use 1 deck
Dealer stands on soft 17
Player can double on any 2 cards
Player can double after split
No surrender rule
Blackjack pays 3 to 2
Dealer does not offer insurance
Can resplit aces
```
